--- authorinformation: [, Kimberley.De.Moor] category: User Guide keyword: --- # Multi-channel publishing Multi-channel publishing is publishing documentation in different output formats, such as webhelp or PDF. One of the great advantages of DITA content is its ability to be published in many different kinds of output formats. Its characteristic re-use strategies along with conditional processing were designed with efficient content-managing and publishing in mind. Essentially, one single source can become many different types of documents. In this section, we will discuss the following topics: - [Structure vs. layout](co_structure_vs__lay_out.html) - [Publishing DITA content](co_publishing.html)
