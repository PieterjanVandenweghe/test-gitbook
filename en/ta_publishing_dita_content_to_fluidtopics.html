--- authorinformation: [, Pieterjan Vandenweghe] keyword: [DITAToo, FluidTopics] --- # Publishing DITA content to FluidTopics 1. Select the DITAMAP you want to publish. 2. In the main menu, choose **FluidTopics** \> **Upload to FluidTopics** 3. Select the correct baseline, languages and conditional profiles and choose **OK**. The content is uploaded to your FluidTopics portal.
